About GoodCacau

GoodCacau is a boutique cacao house committed to the slow, meticulous art of cacao preparation. Our philosophy centers on delivering exceptional quality through handcrafted products, utilizing ethically sourced, single-origin cacao beans from family farms in Bahia. The dedicated team, comprising Marina, Lucas, and Bia, meticulously tempers chocolate in small batches and bakes fresh brownies and brioches daily, ensuring unparalleled freshness and flavor.

Core Values:

•
Authenticity: Prioritizing genuine, high-quality single-origin cacao and natural ingredients.

•
Fair Trade Practices: Ensuring equitable compensation for our cacao farmers, fostering sustainable partnerships.

•
Sustainability: Advocating for eco-conscious practices, exemplified by the use of ceramic mugs over disposable alternatives.

•
Customer Experience: Cultivating a warm, inviting, and tranquil atmosphere for every patron.

Ordering and Logistics

GoodCacau employs a streamlined and personalized ordering system:

1.
Menu Selection: Customers browse the online menu and select desired products.

2.
Order Compilation: Items are added to a virtual order list.

3.
WhatsApp Submission: The compiled order is submitted as a pre-filled message via WhatsApp to the GoodCacau team.

4.
Personalized Confirmation: A team member provides a personalized confirmation via WhatsApp, detailing pickup/delivery timing, product availability, and total cost.

Payment Modalities:
Payments are processed upon pickup or delivery, accommodating Pix, credit/debit card, or cash. Online prepayment is not required.

Delivery and Pickup:
Customers have the option for in-store pickup at our café or local delivery within a defined radius. Delivery fees are communicated during the order confirmation process.
